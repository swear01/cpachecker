## Interpretation

1. On the broad 764-entry Loops ReachSafety set, **v1.4 VGuide still improves the simple predicate baseline**: 262 solved vs 225 solved, net **+37**.
2. The gain is again **TRUE-oriented**: VGuide adds 42 stock-UNKNOWN → TRUE cases and adds **0 new FALSE** cases.
3. VGuide regresses 5 stock TRUE cases to UNKNOWN on this broad set, so the result is not a strict superset of stock.
4. `--svcomp26` is much stronger overall: 486 solved, net **+261** vs stock and **+224** vs v1.4 VGuide. Most of the gap is portfolio/config strength, including 92 additional expected-FALSE solves that current VGuide does not target.
5. The strongest publishable v1.5 claim is therefore not “VGuide beats SV-COMP portfolio”, but: **VGuide provides a measurable +37 solved improvement over the same simple PredicateCPA baseline on a larger official Loops-derived set, with 33 VGuide-only TRUE solves even when compared against `--svcomp26`.**
